What We Do

There is a deep longing in each one of us that wants to connect with the true self, the ‘inner child’ so to speak, the second personality that has been buried away too long, who is crying out for our attention. We feel fragmented and desperately attempt in all sorts of ways to relocate the broken pieces and put back together again a version of ourselves we think we ought to be.
